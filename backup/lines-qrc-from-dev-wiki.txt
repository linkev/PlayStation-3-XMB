ENVIRONMENT VARIATIONS:
The override folder contains environment-specific configurations for:
- Time-based: yoake (dawn), day, higure (sunset), night
- Boot sequences: coldboot1, coldboot2, gameboot1-5, welcome_1-2
- Special modes: black, bright, initial_setting, music_1

TECHNICAL NOTES:
- Wave animation: spline.elf (uses spline wavelet functions)
- Particle system: particles.elf (dynamic physics simulation)
- Background textures: 24 monthly DDS files (12 day + 12 night variations)
- Particle texture: proc_iridescent.tga (procedural iridescent effect)
- UI responds to DualShock controller motion sensors
- Particles react dynamically to XMB cursor movement
- HDR rendering with bloom and glare effects
- FFD (Free Form Deformation) for wave distortion
